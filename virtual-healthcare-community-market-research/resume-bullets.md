## Enhanced Bullets — Strategy / BD / Market Research Role

**Virtual Healthcare Community — Market Research & Strategic Analysis** | Market Research, Strategy
- Conducted quantitative survey with 70+ respondents + 10 qualitative follow-up interviews to evaluate virtual healthcare adoption in India; identified a trust-adoption paradox: 84% awareness vs. 18% regular usage, with doctor credibility (54%) and privacy concerns (49%) as top barriers
- Sized India digital health market: $11.6 Bn TAM → $3.8 Bn SAM (telemedicine, 27% CAGR) → $380 Mn SOM *(assumption-labeled)*; analyzed industry reports from FICCI-EY, NITI Aayog, WHO SEARO, and Statista
- Applied SWOT and PESTEL frameworks across 6 dimensions; identified government ABDM tailwind and Telemedicine Guidelines 2020 as enabling factors; flagged DPDP Act 2023 as key compliance requirement
- Benchmarked 5 platforms (Practo, Apollo 247, Tata Health, 1mg, Tata Health); identified community-first positioning as unoccupied strategic whitespace; developed 4-recommendation strategy targeting trust, community, regulatory alignment, and Tier-2 vernacular expansion
