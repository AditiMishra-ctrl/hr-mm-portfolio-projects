## Enhanced Bullets — HR / Talent Acquisition Role

**Talent Acquisition — Recruitment Funnel & Hiring Process Analysis** | Excel, Process Design
- Mapped 5-stage recruitment funnel (1,000 applications → 18 hires, 1.8% overall conversion); identified 34-day time-to-hire (2.3x industry benchmark of 15 days) and 29.5% Interview→Offer conversion (vs. 40–50% benchmark) as critical failure points
- Benchmarked 4 sourcing channels by quality-of-hire score and cost-per-hire; found employee referrals deliver highest quality (4.3/5 rating) at ₹0 cost vs. LinkedIn (3.8/5, ₹18,000/hire) → recommended ₹10,000 referral incentive (still 44% cheaper than LinkedIn)
- Designed competency-based interview scorecard across 5 dimensions (problem-solving 30%, role skills 25%, communication 20%, culture fit 15%, growth mindset 10%) with behavioral anchors to improve inter-rater reliability from 64% to 85%
- Recommended SLA framework, referral program expansion, and scorecard adoption targeting: time-to-hire <15 days, cost-per-hire from ₹14,400 → ₹9,000 (-38%), offer acceptance rate from 77% → 88% *(assumption-labeled)*
