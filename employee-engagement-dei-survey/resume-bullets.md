## Enhanced Bullets — HR Role

**Employee Engagement & DEI Survey Study & Action Plan** | Survey Design, Google Forms, People Analytics
- Designed a 4-section engagement + DEI survey (eNPS, 6 engagement drivers, 4 manager effectiveness items, 4 DEI perception items) and administered to 50+ employees; achieved eNPS of 0 (vs. +20–40 benchmark), signaling a clear engagement improvement opportunity
- Identified recognition (2.6/5) and growth opportunity (2.7/5) as the two lowest-scoring engagement drivers through Likert analysis; segmented results by tenure and role to find new employees (eNPS: -12) as highest-risk group
- Built a 4-priority, budget-conscious action plan: peer recognition program, transparent promotion criteria, manager development training, and DEI internal mobility initiative — each with specific targets and 90-day pulse survey measurement cadence
- Analyzed 34 open-ended DEI responses; synthesized top themes (unclear promotion criteria: 15/34 mentions, mentorship gaps: 8/34) into concrete policy recommendations targeting promotion fairness score from 2.8 → 3.5
