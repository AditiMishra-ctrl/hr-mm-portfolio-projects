## Enhanced Bullets — HR Analytics / People Analytics Role

**Employee Attrition & Retention Analytics** | People Analytics, Power BI, Excel
- Analyzed 1,470 employee records (IBM HR dataset) across 35 variables using Excel correlation analysis and Power BI segmentation; identified overtime as #1 attrition driver (30.5% vs. 10.4% attrition rate for non-overtime employees)
- Built 5-page interactive Power BI dashboard (overview, risk drivers, segment deep-dive, cost model, at-risk watchlist) enabling HR managers to proactively identify at-risk employees before resignation
- Modeled annual cost of turnover at ₹12.89 Cr (SHRM benchmark: 75% of salary); found that 11% of employees matching 4+ risk criteria drive 41% of all voluntary attrition
- Designed 4 prioritized retention interventions with ROI analysis: overtime policy (₹0 cost, -5–8pp attrition), compensation correction (₹40L investment → ₹1.5 Cr saving), enhanced onboarding, and sales retention program *(assumption-labeled)*
